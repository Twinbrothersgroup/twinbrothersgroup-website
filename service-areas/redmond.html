Framing Built for Redmond's Tech-Driven Growth

Redmond is no longer just the Microsoft campus. The city is undergoing one of the most significant urban transformations in King County -- with the Overlake Village area rapidly densifying, East Link light rail reshaping transit corridors, and a wave of new housing developments responding to tech worker demand. Twin Brothers Group is positioned to frame every type of project this growth creates.

We have experience framing corporate campus buildings and large tech employer office projects in the Redmond area. We have also framed multifamily apartment projects in the Overlake corridor and residential developments on the Sammamish Plateau. From large commercial to high-density multifamily to custom single-family homes, we bring the same discipline and quality across every project type.

Redmond GCs trust us because we understand the pace of tech-driven construction. Schedules are tight, quality standards are high, and there is no tolerance for delays. Twin Brothers Group operates exactly the way our clients need -- fast bids, on-time crews, and framing that passes inspection the first time.
