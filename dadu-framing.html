DADU Framing

A Complete Small Home in Your Backyard

DADUs — detached accessory dwelling units, better known in Seattle as backyard cottages — are one of the fastest-growing residential project types in the region. A DADU is a full standalone structure, which means it gets the complete framing scope of a custom home at small scale: floor system, walls, and roof.

Building in an occupied backyard takes more than framing skill. Material delivery through side yards or alleys, staging on constrained lots, and working around your home, fence, and landscaping all have to be planned before the first stick of lumber arrives. Our crews do this on tight Seattle lots all the time.

Converting space inside your existing home instead? See our ADU framing services for attached units, garage conversions, and basement ADUs.
