Company Overview

Twin Brothers Group LLC is a professional framing subcontractor headquartered in Bellevue, Washington. Founded in 2017, we have built a reputation for precision framing, reliable scheduling, and competitive pricing on multifamily and commercial projects across the Greater Seattle Area.

We specialize in production wood framing for multifamily developments — apartment complexes, townhomes, condos, and mixed-use buildings — and bring the same focus on quality and speed to commercial and residential work. General contractors and developers across King and Snohomish County trust us because we show up on time, frame it right, and don't create problems on the job site.

Twin Brothers Group is family-owned and operated. Every project we take on gets direct attention from ownership — there's no layer of management between the GC and the crew.

Key Differentiators

48-Hour Bid Turnaround

Send us plans today and receive a competitive, itemized bid within 48 hours — often the same day. GCs on tight schedules cannot afford slow subs. We respect your timeline from day one.

Bilingual Crew — English & Spanish

Our foremen and crew are bilingual in English and Spanish. This means clearer communication on the job site, fewer misunderstandings, and tighter quality control — especially on large multifamily projects where coordination is critical.

Licensed & Insured in Washington State

Twin Brothers Group carries full general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage in Washington State. All licensing is current and compliant. Certificates of insurance are available upon request within 24 hours.

Our production framing model is built for volume multifamily work. When your project has repetitive floor plans and high unit counts, we leverage crew efficiency and material volume to deliver pricing that outperforms custom framing subs — without cutting corners on quality.
