Residential Framing

Precision Framing for Your Custom Home

Twin Brothers Group provides expert residential framing for custom homes, spec builds, and production homes throughout the Greater Seattle Area. We work with general contractors, custom builders, and homeowners to deliver clean, code-compliant framing on schedule.

Residential framing requires attention to detail — complex roof lines, cathedral ceilings, intricate floor plans. Our crews have the experience to execute precisely, coordinate with your subs, and pass inspection the first time.

We've been framing homes across King and Snohomish Counties for 7+ years. From the first floor joist to the last ridge board — we treat every home like it's our own.
