Our Story

Built by Brothers. Trusted by GCs.

Twin Brothers Group was founded on a simple idea: bring the work ethic and accountability of a family business to commercial and residential framing. Since 2017, we've grown from a small crew into one of the most trusted framing subcontractors in the Greater Seattle Area.

We specialize in wood framing for general contractors and developers across King, Snohomish, and Pierce Counties. Whether it's a multifamily apartment complex, a commercial office building, or a custom single-family home, we show up with the right crew, the right tools, and a commitment to getting it done right.

Our reputation is built on speed, precision, and communication — the three things GCs tell us matter most when they're coordinating a job site. That's why they call us first.
