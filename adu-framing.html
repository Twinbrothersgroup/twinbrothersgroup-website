ADU Framing

Add Living Space — and Value — to Your Property

Accessory dwelling units are booming across the Greater Seattle Area — and for good reason. An ADU adds rental income, gives multigenerational families room to live together, and Washington State's recent ADU legislation has made permitting easier than ever in King and Snohomish County cities.

Framing an ADU is different from framing a new house. You're adding load to an existing structure — tying new walls into old framing, cutting headers for new openings, and building floor systems over garages. That's where experience matters, and that's exactly what Twin Brothers Group brings to every ADU project.

Building a standalone unit in your backyard instead? See our DADU framing services for detached backyard cottages.
